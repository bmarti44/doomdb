## Route and boundary

`T4.1-EVAL | Sol | high | attempt 1`

The evaluator fixes three table SQL macro interfaces: `DOOM_R1_RAYS`,
`DOOM_R1_HITS`, and `DOOM_R1_NEAREST`, each bound to a session and its current
player. It requires 320 half-pixel, unnormalized camera rays; indexed Spatial
frustum candidate reduction; exact Appendix B intersection; stable
`(t, linedef_id, seg_id, facing_side)` ordering; facing sidedef resolution; and
nearest solid selection after open portals.

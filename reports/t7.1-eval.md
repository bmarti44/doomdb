`DOOM_COMBAT.ADVANCE(session_token,tic)` procedure, delegated by the owning tic

interactive E1M1 thing types, including all weapon/pickup types and barrel 2035;

machinery, performs fixed ordered spread/damage RNG reads, and cannot penetrate
actors or blocking openings. Projectiles use swept collision. Splash has bounded
distance falloff and line-of-effect occlusion. Destroyed barrels chain in stable
mobj order, once each. State/refire/flash timing, event ordinals, inventory,
projectile rows, RNG cursor, rollback, and session isolation are authoritative.
